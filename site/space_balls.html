Spaceballs: the Penny Arcade Game

THE GAME

This game is a first person space balls (asteroids) simulator. Use your laser turret to demolish the space balls coming at your ship before they demolish you! As time goes on, the balls become more and more hectic and you have less time to clear them before they finish off your ship. Do you have the space balls to survive?s

The asteroids are the property of father's brother's nephew's cousin's former roommate. What's that make them to you?
Absolutely nothing! Which is what they are about to become!

Use your schwartz-powered turret to fire at the asteroids coming at your winnebago! Your crew is doing repairs as you fly through this asteroid field, and they only need the ship to survive sufficiently long enough. Shooting asteroids gives your crew more time to repair!

You have the ring, and I see your Schwartz is as big as mine. Now let's see how well you handle it.

Game has levels. Shooting asteroids increases your time remaining. Each level launches balls faster and ticks down faster. The final boss sends balls flying around at ludicrous speed!
It is quite possible to lose! Losing conditions: run out of time (Missing a sufficent number of balls)
Say goodbye to your two best friends, and I don't mean your pals in the Winnebago.
The turret has a delay in its firing state. When the trigger is detected, a shot is made. Raspberry Pi determins if a hit is made by checking the brightness in the center area. 
            ##### snippet #####
If hit, tells game state to increase time remaining. Depending on color, offers different power ups.
Neopixels show the remaining time in a level. 
            ##### NEOPIXEL CODE SEGMENT #####
State holder pix with many utility functions to write to it.
